Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that 
enables machines to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence. It includes abilities such as learning from data, recognizing patterns, understanding 
language, and 
making decisions. AI systems use algorithms and models to process large amounts of information quickly and 
accurately. Today, AI is used in many applications like virtual assistants, recommendation
 systems, and self-driving cars. It helps
  businesses improve efficiency and 
  provides smarter solutions to complex 
  problems.

AI is transforming industries such as healthcare, finance, education, and transportation. In healthcare, AI helps doctors diagnose diseases and predict patient 
outcomes. In finance, it detects fraud 
and automates trading decisions. 
In education, AI-powered tools 
personalize learning experiences for 
students. As technology advances, AI 
continues to grow, creating new 
opportunities and challenges for society.
